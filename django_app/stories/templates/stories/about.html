What is NAHB?

NAHB (Not Another Hero's Book) is an interactive storytelling platform inspired by classic "Choose Your Own Adventure" books. Our platform allows authors to create branching narrative stories and readers to experience them through meaningful choices.

Features
  • Interactive Stories: Make choices that affect the story's outcome
  • Multiple Endings: Discover different paths and endings
  • Dice Rolls: Some choices involve chance with dice mechanics
  • Auto-Save: Your progress is saved automatically
  • Story Visualization: See the story tree and your path
  • Community: Rate, comment, and share your favorite stories
Architecture

NAHB uses a two-tier microservices architecture:

  • Flask API: Handles story content storage (stories, pages, choices)
  • Django App: Manages user experience (authentication, gameplay, community)
Design Patterns
  • Singleton: FlaskAPIClient - Single API connection instance
  • Factory: StoryFactory - Story creation with validation
  • Mediator: GameMediator - Coordinates gameplay components
  • Memento: GameStateMemento - Saves and restores game state
