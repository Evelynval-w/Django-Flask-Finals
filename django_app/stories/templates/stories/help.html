How do I play a story?

Browse stories, click "View Story", then click "Play" to start. Make choices by clicking the buttons to progress through the story.

What are dice rolls?

Some choices require a dice roll. You'll need to roll a certain number or higher to succeed. If you fail, you may need to try a different path.

Is my progress saved?

Yes! Your progress is automatically saved. You can close the browser and continue later.

How do I rate a story?

After viewing a story, you can rate it from 1-5 stars. You must be logged in to rate.

How do I create a story?

Go to Author Dashboard ‚Üí Create New Story. Fill in the title, description, and optionally add a cover image URL.

How do I add pages?

After creating a story, click "+ Add Page". Write your content and mark if it's an ending page.

How do I connect pages with choices?

On any page, use the "Add Choice" section. Enter the choice text and select which page it leads to.

How do I add dice rolls?

When creating a choice, check "Dice required" and set the minimum roll needed (1-6).

How do I publish my story?

Stories start as drafts. Click "Publish" when your story is ready for readers.

User Roles
  • Reader: Browse and play stories, rate, comment
  • Author: All reader abilities + create and manage stories
  • Admin: All abilities + moderate content and users
How do I become an author?

Contact an administrator to upgrade your account to author status.

How do I report inappropriate content?

On any story page, click "Report" and describe the issue. Our moderation team will review it.

What happens after I report?

Admins review reports and may suspend stories that violate guidelines.
